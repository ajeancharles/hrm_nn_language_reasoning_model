EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on August 26, 2025 between 
TechCorp Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Company"), and Employee 22 ("Employee").

WHEREAS, Company desires to employ Employee in the position of Software Engineer;
WHEREAS, Employee agrees to perform services for Company under the terms herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, 
the parties agree as follows:

1. EMPLOYMENT AND DUTIES
   Employee shall serve as Software Engineer and shall perform duties including:
   - Software development and coding
   - Code review and testing
   - Collaboration with development team
   - Documentation and maintenance

2. COMPENSATION
   Company shall pay Employee an annual salary of $120,000, payable in accordance 
   with Company's regular payroll practices.

3. BENEFITS
   Employee shall be eligible for:
   - Health insurance coverage
   - 401(k) retirement plan participation
   - Paid time off according to Company policy
   - Professional development opportunities

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Employee acknowledges access to confidential information and agrees to maintain 
   strict confidentiality both during and after employment.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
   All work product developed during employment shall be owned by Company.
   Employee assigns all rights in such work product to Company.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION
   Employment is at-will and may be terminated by either party with two weeks notice.
   Upon termination, Employee shall return all Company property.

7. NON-COMPETE
   For twelve months after termination, Employee shall not engage in competing 
   business activities within the same geographic market.

8. GOVERNING LAW
   This Agreement shall be governed by Delaware law.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement.
