ITEM 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

On August 26, 2025, Alphabet Inc. entered into a significant commercial agreement 
with a major technology partner. This agreement establishes the terms and conditions for 
a strategic partnership that will enable both companies to leverage their respective 
strengths in the marketplace.

The key terms of this agreement include:

WHEREAS, the Company seeks to expand its market presence through strategic partnerships;
WHEREAS, the counterparty possesses complementary technology and market access;
WHEREAS, both parties desire to create synergistic value through collaboration;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. SCOPE OF PARTNERSHIP: The parties shall collaborate on joint product development 
   and market expansion initiatives.

2. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY: Each party retains ownership of its pre-existing intellectual 
   property while jointly developed IP shall be shared according to contribution.

3. FINANCIAL TERMS: Revenue sharing shall be calculated based on predefined metrics 
   and performance milestones.

4. TERM AND TERMINATION: This agreement shall remain in effect for an initial term 
   of three years, with automatic renewal provisions.

The agreement contains customary representations, warranties, and indemnification provisions. 
The Company believes this partnership will create significant value for shareholders through 
enhanced market opportunities and operational synergies.

This agreement was negotiated at arm's length and was approved by the Company's board of 
directors following review by legal counsel.
